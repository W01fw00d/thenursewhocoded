### Why?

The ultimate goal of GTD is not really to be productive at all times, it's more about achieving ease of mind ğŸ˜Œ. You see, when you have chores and other pending obligations in your mind, it is hard to relax or even focus on your current and more urgent task ğŸš¨.

You write them down in a way you can come back and pick them up easily later when you are free ğŸ¤—. That way you prioritize what's more urgent, and you can forget about them and experience some ["Carpe diem"](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Carpe_diem).
